Why Proper Disposal Matters

Environmental Damage

Lithium and heavy metals can leach into soil and groundwater, contaminating ecosystems for decades. Improper disposal contributes to toxic landfill accumulation.

Resource Waste

Smartphones contain precious metals like gold,silver,and rare earth.Throwing them away wastes these finite resources that require energy-intensive mining.

Health Risks

Exposure to electronic waste components can cause respiratory issues,skin irritation,and long-term health problems in communities near disposal sites.

How and Where to Dispose

Electronics recycling centers
  • Remove all personal data before disposal
  • Take to certified e-waste recycling facility
